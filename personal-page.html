About Me

Just a few things about me. I am 34 years old. I was born in Minnesota. I have lived here in Minnesota my entire life. I have a 8 year old Husky mix named Luna.

Some of my favorite activies include:

  • Video Games

    • I spend most of my free time playing video games with my friends. Down below is a list of some of my favorite games. I have even built my own gaming desktop. Check out my photos page!

  • Hockey

    • I love the sport of hockey! I started playing hockey through a local organization HockeyFinder about 10 years ago. I never got the chance to play growing up, it is a pretty expensive sport. I am glad I get to play now, it is a great workout and a lot of fun! I also love watching Hockey. The Minnesota Wild is my favorite team. Hopefully they actually do something this year!

  • Fishing

    • I grew up fishing from time to time. As I got older I didn't have the opportunity and went a long time without fishing. Last year I bought myself a fishing boat and enjoy taking Luna out to attempt to catch some fish.

  • Hunting

    • Hunting is a bit of a new experience for me. Growing up I always wanted to go deer hunting but never had the option. My family wasn't into hunting so I had no opportunity. A few years ago I decided to do a lot of research, watch a lot of Youtube, and take on the adventure myself. So far I am unsuccessful.

  • Breweries

    • I love beer! It is pretty convenient that I live near Minneapolis with how many breweries there are. I like a large variety of beers but blondes, ambers, and Oktoberfest style beers tend to be my favorite.

  • Twitch

    • Twitch streaming service is a great place to go to relax and watch video games be played. Some of the streams I watch are related to one of my favorite games (DOTA2). My favorite DOTA2 streams to watch are usually the tournaments, watching pro teams compete in large LAN or online tournaments.
